SKYRIM

This is an open-world action role-playing game developed and published by Bethesda. This game is the fifth installment to the Elder Scroll Series created by Bethesda. The story focuses on the player's character who is revealed to be what is known as, Dragonborn and thier quest is to defeat the evil dragon, Alduin The World-Eater. The player will travel all around Skyrim completing quest and meeting new characters in order to improve their skills and level up as they progress through the story.
